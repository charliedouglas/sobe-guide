Know Before You Go

~ the useful bits ~
👕 The dress-code cheat sheet: Days & beach = wear anything, kits welcome. Sunset rooftops = smart-casual, drop the kit (plain tee or shirt). Big clubs (LIV / E11EVEN / Story) = trousers or dark denim + collared shirt + leather shoes. No football shirts, no shorts, no trainers — this door is strict and they will turn you away.
🌡️ The heat is the real opponent: June in SoBe is hot AND brutally humid even at midnight. Linen/cotton outside; the clubs crank AC hard so you'll swing from sweating to shivering. Lean into colour — Miami rewards a bit of flash.
⚽ World Cup crush: beachfront spots (esp. Nikki) get rammed on match days. Backyard and High Tide stay more civilised. Rep Scotland at the fan zones / Ocean Drive sports bars by day — but change before the evening clubs (shirts won't get you in).
🚕 Getting about: most daytime + sunset spots are walkable along Collins/Ocean/Washington. E11EVEN is Downtown — grab a quick Uber over.
A note on prices & DJs: entry, bottle and drink prices in South Beach shift constantly and most venues don't publish them — figures marked "~" are typical ranges to budget around, not quotes. Day-pass and ticket prices are from recent listings and may change. Club DJ line-ups are announced week-to-week, so check each venue's site/socials closer to your trip for who's actually playing.
